Join the APT Game Generator now and begin your journey in gamedev!

To become an official member of APT GameGenerator:

1. Fill the application form: [https://goo.gl/forms/1Xpsq7tOVzNP50F53](https://goo.gl/forms/1Xpsq7tOVzNP50F53)
1. Wait for reply.
1. Get accepted!

Member rights:
\- your name in the member list on the APT GG home page (optional)
\- participation in various events at a discounted price or free
\- discounted transport to various events
\- development and marketing support for your game project by APT GG

Our community and the events are open for everyone interested in gamedev! If you have no previous experience, then all the better - you will have a great time discovering everything together with us!

Join in the [APT GG-s Facebook group here](https://www.facebook.com/groups/GameGenerator/).

See you soon! :)
