During the [Tartu Student Days](http://studentdays.ee/) in the Fall 2016 we hosted two [Unity Game Dev Workshops](https://www.facebook.com/events/314674858868181/). In those workshops everyone interested in learning the Unity game engine could come and take their first steps in making games with it. On each day we had about 15 to 20 participants of varying ages and backgrounds.

The workshop was put together and organized by our members [Jaanus Jaggo](https://www.facebook.com/jaanus.jaggo),  [Marko Korbun](https://www.facebook.com/marko.korbun) and [Raul Kübarsepp](https://www.facebook.com/raul.kubarsepp). Thanks to all the students who participated!
