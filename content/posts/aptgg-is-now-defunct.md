APT Game Generator is no longer active.

Between 2016 and 2024 our community in Tartu organized game jams, weekly workshops and talks — from Ludum Dare gatherings and Global Game Jam sites to Unity and Godot workshops. Dozens of games were made, and many people got their first taste of game development here.

This site remains online as an archive of what we did together. Browse the [game jams](/ludum-dare/), the [projects](/projects/), or any of the event posts to see the games and the photos.

Thank you to everyone who jammed, presented, mentored, volunteered or just showed up on a Monday evening.
