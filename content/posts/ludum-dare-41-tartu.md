54 jammers + 92 pizzas + [110 energy drinks](http://www.alecoq.ee/en/product/dynami-t-white-energy/) = 13 games in one weekend!This Ludum Dare theme was really interesting: Combine 2 Incompatible Genres.
The theme produced the strangest games in any game jam we have hosted so far. Oddly enough, most of them related to dating.

And we got extremely lucky. We had a mentor with 20 years of game development with a massive amount of experience, Mika Tammenkoski.

As usual, at the end of the event 3 winners were chosen. [GoryTale (I place)](https://ldjam.com/events/ludum-dare/41/gorytale) It’s a mix of two absolutely different game worlds - fairy tales meet brutality and gore. Fairy tale world settings meet “think Duke Nukem, Doomguy, Serious Sam” with their bloodthirsty habits destroying nice things.![IMG_2604](/app/uploads/2018/05/IMG_2604.webp) [Wonky Farm Dance Party (II place)](https://ldjam.com/events/ludum-dare/41/$88613/) Is a rhythmic puzzle platformer plot generator!!!You are a farmer infected by the dancing plague. The farm still needs to be managed, however, and the farmland protected from the Elements.
